Tourist Places in New York

Central Park

Central Park is an urban oasis in the heart of Manhattan. Visitors can enjoy activities such as boating, picnicking, and exploring its many trails and attractions.

Times Square

Times Square is known for its dazzling lights, Broadway theaters, and vibrant atmosphere. It's a must-visit destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy. Visitors can take a ferry to Liberty Island to get up close to this iconic monument and learn about its history.
